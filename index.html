This is a interdepartment collaborative project between the Department of Curriculum and Instruction, Department of Hispanic Studies of University of Houston and Department of World Languages of University of North Texas

My role in the project were to develop the overall design of the website, structure of the contents, development of th interactive activities and the back-end interface for content maintenance. Skills utilized in the project were...

Powerful Tools for Teaching and Learning: Web 2.0 Tools MOOC  January 2014 - March 2015

About the Project

This MOOC (Massive Open Online Course) focuses on learning innovative Web 2.0 tools in K-12 instruction and effectively integrating them into classroom practices as well as creating engaging student activities. The platform used in this MOOC was Coursera.

My role in the project were to develop the overall design of the course, structure of the contents, development of problem-scenario videos and other multimedia contents such as related videos and graphics. Skills utilized in the project were...

  • Instructional design using ADDIE and Rapid Prototyping method.
  • Project management of the materials in collaboration with the subject-matter experts.
  • Content development using storyboarding and scriptwriting
  • Content design using HTML, CSS and Bootstrap 2.
  • Video development using Primiere and Primiere Elements.
  • Graphic design using Photoshop.
  • Content organization and implementation using Coursera.
